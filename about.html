About

Hello! I'm Stephen Perona, freelance animator and 3D modeler, currently living in the Willamette Valley of Oregon. Since first being introduced to 3D modeling in 2002, I have worked tirelessly to teach myself the ins-and-outs of the craft, initially utilizing Autodesk's 3D Studio Max software. Being predominately self-taught, I relied on online communities to grow my experience and gain exposure. I've participated in community projects as well as several online competitions, twice resulting in features in "3D Creative" magazine.

In 2006, I began using Autodesk's Maya and directed my focus towards learning character animation. In 2008, I participated in the Mass Animation project, a crowd sourced animation project presented on Facebook. In the end, four of my submitted shots were selected for inclusion in the final short film entitled "Live Music". The film made the rounds at film festivals, and was released on iTunes and theatrically as the opener to "Planet 51" at select locations.

In addition to working as a freelance animator and 3D modeler, I recently graduated from Oregon State University with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and do some work as a software/web programmer.

Feel free to use the contact page to contact me regarding any comments, critiques, or job opportunities!
